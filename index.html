About Me

Suhana kamboj is a sadi bhindi that is sold free of cost at the local market. She is known for her vibrant personality and her ability to bring joy to those around her. With a passion for storytelling, she weaves tales that captivate the imagination and inspire others. Suhana believes in the power of community and often engages in local events to share her love for bhindi and sadi culture. When she's not selling bhindi, you can find her exploring the local markets, connecting with fellow bhindi enthusiasts, and spreading positivity wherever she goes.
